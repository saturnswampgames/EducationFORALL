OUR MOTTO
Helping Others Is How We Thrive.

Slum Kids are not able to study by themselves even if they have books, because mostly there books are too old or they can't understabd what's written in it. With your help and some valuable time they can learn to read and write by themselves, giving them an endless possiblity for their future. Helping them will nourish their wings which will then help them to fly harder and stronger.

Our small efforts and step can make a whole lot of different for them. We will be able to help them to go to school someday and then college. The feeling of someone achieving something great because of you is a feeling that cannot be measured. The joy the pride that our hearts have felt helping these kids is immeasurable till date. Now i ask you to help them and savour that felling for youself.




It is alright if you can't teach them, there are many other ways with which you can help them. One of those ways being donating your old books to our foundation, so that we can distribute it further ahead. It doesn't matter to what classes these books belong to. here we have never enough
